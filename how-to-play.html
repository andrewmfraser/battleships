How to play

1. Placing your ships

You can place your ships either vertically or horizontally. You choose this by clicking the link at the bottom of the page which will say "switch to horizontal" or "switch to vertical".

You then click on the square you want your ship to go, the ship will extend to the squares as indicated by the amber dots.

2. Taking shots

To take shots at the computers ships simply click on the square you want your shot to go on the left grid labeled computer board.

The winner is the player who sinks all of his or hers opponents ships. You can see the health of your ships (bottom right) and the computers ships (bottom left).

3. Play it

To get started click "lets play!" at the bottom of this screen
